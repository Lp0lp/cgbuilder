How to build beads:
  • Click an atom to add it to the current bead (clicking again increases its weight).
  • Shift + Click an atom to decrease its weight / remove it from the current bead.
  • New bead creates a fresh bead and selects it.
  • Rename beads on the right; exports and previews update automatically.
